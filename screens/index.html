    • ARTICLE: MY GOALS AND TARGET FOR THE HNG INTERNSHIP

    Written By: Oscar Anyiam

    The HNG internship is one of the most anticipated internships in the country. It presents the opportunity for tech enthusiasts who want to get started in tech careers, HNG helps to bring out the potential in you. I opted for the internship immediately after just concluding the Zuri Internship, this is as a result of my drive and determination to be skilled in my chosen track. Mobile development with flutter gives developer the ability the write once and run for various platforms. This is a powerful innovation which saves the time and effort of managing multiple code bases. Flutter is a multiplatform frame work that will allow me build robust and efficient mobile application for the android and ios platform.

    The Internship is scheduled to run for 8 weeks, this is an exciting and thrilling journey which I am gainfully prepared for. I have read up articles and tutorials to get me up to speed with the basics of building mobile application with flutter, also my recently concluded internship at Zuri has prepared me for the HNG internship. Below is my 8weeks timeline strategy on how I plan to get to the final stage

    During this period I plan to achieve the following:

    • Develop myself and increase my network
    • Practice basic software principles and algorithms
    • Develop sound mobile applications
    • Have fun while learning
    • Learn team leadership skills

      Outlined Goals

    • Get to the final stage of the internship and win a shirt
    • Develop mobile apps and publish to the app store
    • Get a paid internship or job in a top software firm
